The Secret of Becoming Mentally Strong

发表于 2018-12-04 | 分类于 精神食粮 | 已有 次浏览

推荐一个TED演讲，The Secret of Becoming Mentally Strong怎样成为一个精神强大的人。

时长大约15分钟，其中两段话印象深刻，很受用，记录下来。

When you’re really going through tough times, good habits aren’t enough. It only takes one or two small habits to really hold you back.
当你真的在经历困难的时候，好的习惯是不够的。只需要一两个小的坏习惯就能真的让你退缩。

Unhealthy beliefs about ourselves mostly come about because we’re uncomfortable with our feelings. Feeling sad, or hurt, or angry, or scared, those things are all uncomfortable. So we go to great lengths to avoid that discomfort. We try to escape it by doing things like hosting a pity party. And although that’s a temporary distraction, it just prolongs the pain. The only way to get through uncomfortable emotions, the only way to deal with them, is you have to go through them. To let yourself feel sad, and then move on.
对自己不健康的认知主要来源于我们对自己不舒服的感觉。觉得悲伤，受伤，愤怒，害怕，这些事情都是不舒服的。所以我们尽了最大的努力来避免这种不适。我们会试着通过不断怜悯自己来逃避。尽管这只是暂时分散注意力，但它只会延长痛苦。客服不舒服情绪的唯一方法，也是处理它们的唯一方法，就是你必须经历它们。让自己感到悲伤，然后继续前进。
